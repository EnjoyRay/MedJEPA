# Paper Figure Manifest

- `results\paper_figures\fig0_perturbation_visual_examples.png`: Visual examples of the exact nuisance perturbation schedule applied to one raw chest X-ray.
- `results\paper_figures\fig1_exp1_robustness_matrix.png`: Exp1 macro AUROC and cosine drift curves for I-JEPA-H/95, I-JEPA-H/201, MAE-H/97, and MAE-H/300.
- `results\paper_figures\fig2_exp1_drop_drift_tradeoff.png`: Scatter plot showing that representation drift and downstream AUROC drop are related but not interchangeable.
- `results\paper_figures\table1_exp1_complete_summary.png`: Complete Exp1 robustness table with best AUROC highlighted per condition.
- `results\paper_figures\fig3_exp2_occlusion_summary.png`: Exp2 original AUROC, lesion/control AUROC drops, and lesion-control AUROC delta.
- `results\paper_figures\fig4_exp2_paired_effects.png`: Per-sample lesion-control deltas with 95% CIs for I-JEPA-H/95, I-JEPA-H/201, and MAE-H/97.
- `results\paper_figures\fig5_exp2_per_sample_distribution.png`: Per-sample distributions and paired scatter plots for max-class prediction drop.
- `results\paper_figures\fig6_exp2_subgroup_effects.png`: Subgroup deltas by bbox area and disease group.
- `results\paper_figures\fig7_exp2_per_class_heatmap.png`: Per-class signed score-drop differences between lesion and control occlusion.
- `results\paper_figures\fig8_case_study_contact_sheet.png`: Representative qualitative cases from the generated case-study images.
