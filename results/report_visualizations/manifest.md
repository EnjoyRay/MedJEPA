# JEPA300 Report Visualization Manifest

- `results/report_visualizations/fig_input_noise_occlusion_examples.png`: Input-level visual examples of Gaussian noise and class-aligned lesion/control occlusion.
- `results/report_visualizations/fig_input_perturbation_grid.png`: Input-level examples for the full Exp1 nuisance perturbation family.
- `results/report_visualizations/fig0_jepa300_fair_dashboard.png`: One-page visual summary of the fair JEPA300-vs-MAE300 rerun.

- `results/report_visualizations/fig_exp1_full_perturbation_heatmap.png`: Full Exp1 perturbation matrix covering noise, blur, brightness, and contrast.
- `results/report_visualizations/fig_exp2b_jepa300_mae300_lesion.png`: Overall and per-class class-aligned lesion sensitivity for Exp2b.
- `results/report_visualizations/fig_exp2b_per_sample_distribution.png`: Per-sample Exp2b lesion-control delta distributions and area/class breakdowns.
- `results/report_visualizations/fig_exp3_jepa300_mae300_frequency.png`: Frequency-domain vulnerability comparison for Exp3.
- `results/report_visualizations/fig_exp3_per_class_frequency_heatmap.png`: Per-class AUROC heatmap under the Exp3 mid-frequency corruption band.
- `results/report_visualizations/fig_exp4_jepa300_mae300_mechanism.png`: Mechanism metrics by disease group for Exp4.
- `results/report_visualizations/fig_exp4_per_sample_mechanism_scatter.png`: Per-sample Exp4 mechanism scatter plots.
- `results/report_visualizations/fig_exp5_exp6_jepa300_mae300_mitigation.png`: Preprocessing and NCA mitigation visual summary for Exp5/Exp6.
- `results/report_visualizations/fig_exp6_nca_training_details.png`: NCA loss curves and raw-vs-adapted drift compression details.
- `results/report_visualizations/fig_exp7_exp8_jepa300_mae300_capacity.png`: Linear, MLP, and partial-finetuning Gaussian-noise curves for Exp7/Exp8.
